Target Audience

The target audience of the Mayab Chamber of Commerce are entrepreneurs, owners of small, medium, or big businesses in order to increase productivity.

Two Personas

Persona 1

For all artisans, service providers, merchants, and farmers who want to make themselves known in a competitive market and be able to position their products and services in the state market.

Persona 2

People who are looking for high-quality products or services, since they can find a vast variety of suppliers and can even have a national or international reach since, being a port city, it is very visited, in fact in 2019 Yucatan had a record of visits of 3.2 million visitors so easily these people are looking for products and services.

Scenarios

Scenario 1
I am visiting the city where can I buy handicrafts?
Scenario 2
I would like to import kanasin products, with whom can I buy them and what would be the cost?
Scenario 3
I want to eat the typical foods of the region where to find the restaurants?
Scenario 4
People who want to visit and know the tourist areas and know where they can spend the night where they can eat etc.
Scenario 5
I am planning my coming vacation. I need information to choose clothes and buy them in the city that I am going to visit.
